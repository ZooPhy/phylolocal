# Third-party notices

PhyloLocal vendors a minimal subset of D3 modules so the viewer can run without fetching dependencies.

Included modules:

- d3-selection 3.0.0
- d3-zoom 3.0.0
- d3-dispatch 3.0.1
- d3-interpolate 3.0.1
- portions of d3-drag 3.0.0
- a local transition interrupt shim in place of the full d3-transition package
